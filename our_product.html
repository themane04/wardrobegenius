WardrobeGenius

WardrobeGenius is more than just an app; it's your personal style assistant that provides outfit ideas based on the clothing you already have in your closet. Using artificial intelligence, the app learns your unique style and suggests outfits tailored to you. Discover a new world of fashion with WardrobeGenius and always look your best without the headache. Say goodbye to the stress of choosing outfits and embrace a streamlined, fashionable lifestyle with WardrobeGenius.

Key Features

Closet Scanner: A feature that allows you to take photos of your clothing items, which are then automatically categorized.

Weather Integration: Outfit suggestions tailored to current or forecasted weather conditions.

Social Sharing: The ability to share outfits or seek feedback from friends within the app.

Shopping Assistant: Recommendations for missing clothing items or accessories that fit your style, complete with links to online stores.

Event-specific Outfits: Outfit suggestions for special occasions such as parties, business meetings, or sporting events.

Style Challenges: Daily or weekly challenges that encourage users to try out new style combinations.

Lookbook: A section where you can save your favorite outfits for easy access later on.

Sustainability Score: A rating that shows how sustainable your selected outfits are, based on material, manufacturing process, etc.

Analytics and Trends: An overview of your most-worn outfits, colors, and brands to better understand your fashion trends.
